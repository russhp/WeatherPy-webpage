Cloudiness' Dependency on Humidity

There is a predictable trend of increasing cloudiness with increasing humidity, as can be seen by the cluster of datapoints in the upper-right corner of the Humidity vs. Cloudiness figure, but there is also a surprising proportion of points that have high humidity with little or no clouds.
